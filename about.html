Playing since 1954 About the Owner

Larson Roberts began to play jazz trumpet when he was 16 in Austin, Texas at his local high school. Under mentor Gilbert Eloreaga, he became lead trumpet at his school and competed nationally. Once out of college, he moved to California to realize his dream of owning a jazz club. This is the first dry bar jazz club in the country.
