New Performances Every Week

Boyd's is an exclusively jazz lounge featuring local musicians. Free of televisions you can focus on music, atmosphere and conversation. Join us at the clubs to hear this unique music live, or listen to Boyd's Radio. Allow our respectfully attired but unpretentious staff to serve you and your companions. We know your favorite part will be our dry bar. Please check our Calendar Page for our jazz performance schedule.

Our Rich History

Boyd's has a rich and vibrant history in the heart of Los Angeles, located just next to the stunning coastline. The club first opened its doors in the 1950s as a small, intimate venue for jazz musicians to showcase their talent. Over the years, Boyd's has become a must-visit destination for jazz lovers from all around the world, attracting some of the biggest names in the industry. Throughout its history, Boyd's has played an essential role in promoting jazz and preserving its rich legacy. Today, the club continues to be a hub of creativity and a vital part of the vibrant Los Angeles jazz scene. With its cozy atmosphere, exceptional music, and stunning location, Boyd's is a true gem of the West Coast.
